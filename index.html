Why Orsus

Orsus is a free-to-use tech platform powered by proprietary blockchain technology where fans, leading artists, and industry newcomers can interact and create a network ownership effect. By implementing agreed-upon smart contracts, the Orsus platform provides the highest level of transparency and immutability in talent discovery and intellectual property tokenization.

The Orsus Ecosytem

Orsuschain

A proprietary hybrid chain that uses PoS and connects with a public chain enabling instant, secure, almost free transactions.

Orxchange

A built-in exchange for fans, emerging talents, and leading artists to trade tokens and fund projects in a vibrant secondary market.

DApps

An open platform featuring both native and free plug-ins for any 3rd party DApps that enables talent discovery and IP tokenization.

The Orsus platform enables anyone to safely vote on talent shows, crowdfund art projects, and receive payments.
